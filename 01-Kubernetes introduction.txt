What is Kubernetes?
Kubernetes is a container management/orchestration tool. It is used when our application is distributed in multiple containers.Its job is to monitor, scale and restart the containers automatically even if they spread across multiple nodes.

It is developed by Google Lab and donated to CNCF-Cloud Native Computing Foundation

Now it is a open source.

Container management activities
1.Deploy
2.Schedule
3.Scale
4.Load balancing

Docker--it will create containers
Kubernetes-manage containers

Features of Kubernetes
1.Automatic bin packing
2.Service discovery and load balancing
3.Storage archestration
4.Self healing
